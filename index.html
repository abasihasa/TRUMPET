The note behind the name

Born in the vibrant streets of Tirana, TRUMPET blends the warmth of Mediterranean flavors with the electric pulse of urban nightlife. Our space echoes with brass notes, clinking glasses, and laughter — a sanctuary for those who seek elegance without pretension.

Inspired by the golden age of jazz bars and the lush green hills of Albania, every corner is wrapped in deep emerald and accented with shimmering gold. Whether you're here for a slow Negroni or a fiery dinner party, TRUMPET hits the right chord.

— The Trumpet family
